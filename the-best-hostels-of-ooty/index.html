Exploring Nature and Budget-Friendly Accommodation in Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a charming hill station located in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. With its lush greenery, cool climate, and stunning landscapes, Ooty is a popular destination for budget travelers looking to explore nature and enjoy outdoor activities such as trekking, boating, and sightseeing. The city is known for its picturesque tea plantations, beautiful gardens, and serene lakes, making it a perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful and budget-friendly escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Ooty also offers affordable accommodation options such as hostels, making it an ideal choice for travelers on a budget.
