The Best Hostels in Ooty for Backpackers

Ooty, nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Whether you're looking to explore the lush tea plantations, trek through scenic landscapes, or simply relax and soak in the mountain views, Ooty has something for every traveler. But where should you stay? We've rounded up the best hostels in Ooty to ensure you have a comfortable, budget-friendly, and memorable trip.

1. Zostel Ooty

Location: Elk Hill, Ooty | 0.6 km away from city center

Why we love it: Zostel Ooty offers a cozy and vibrant atmosphere perfect for meeting fellow travelers. With comfortable dorms and private rooms, this hostel provides stunning views of the surrounding hills. The common areas are great for socializing, and the staff organizes regular activities like bonfires and local tours.

Price Range: ₹500 - ₹1500 per night

Amenities:

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Common lounge
  • On-site café
  • Organized events and activities

Rating: 8.9/10

Book Now or want to check the events? Zostel Ooty

2. Hosteller Ooty

  • Location: Lower Bazaar Road, 1.55 km away from city central
  • Why we love it: Hosteller Ooty is known for its friendly atmosphere and focus on sustainability. They offer clean and comfortable dorms and private rooms, along with a rooftop cafe with stunning views. They also organize social events and eco-friendly activities.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹400 - ₹1200 per night.
  • Amenities:
    • Free Wi-Fi
    • Common area
    • Rooftop cafe
    • Social events and activities
    • Eco-friendly initiatives
  • Rating: Guest ratings online average around 8.5/10.
  • Website: Check their website or booking platforms for more information and booking options.

3. GoStops Ooty

  • Location: Coonoor Road, 2.11 km away from city central
  • Why we love it: GoStops Ooty provides a modern and social hostel experience. They have spacious dorms and private rooms, along with a game room, a co-working space, and a cafe serving delicious meals. They frequently host events and parties.
  • Price Range: Prices typically range from ₹600 - ₹1800 per night.
  • Amenities:
    • Free Wi-Fi
    • Common area
    • Game room
    • Co-working space
    • Cafe
    • Events and parties
  • Rating: Online guest reviews average around 8.7/10.
  • Website: Visit their website or booking platforms for details and booking options.

4. Prabhu Hostels

  • Location: Charring Cross, | 1.99km away from City Central
  • Why we love it: Prabhu Hostels offers a budget-friendly option for travelers in Ooty. They have basic dorm beds and private rooms with a focus on cleanliness and functionality. The location is convenient for exploring the town center.
  • Price Range: This is a budget-friendly option, typically costing around ₹300 - ₹800 per night.
  • Amenities:
    • Limited information available online, but likely includes basic amenities like Wi-Fi and a common area.
  • Rating: Guest reviews online are scarcer, but ratings seem to average around 7.5/10.
  • Contact: Since information online is limited, consider contacting the hostel directly for details and booking options.

Tips for Hostel Travelers in Ooty

  • Best Time to Visit: Ooty is pleasant year-round, but the best time to visit is from October to June.
  • Getting Around: Local buses and auto-rickshaws are affordable ways to get around. Renting a bike is also a great option.
  • Must-See Attractions: Don’t miss the Ooty Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak, and a ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.
  • Cultural Etiquette: Respect local customs and dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.

Exploring Ooty is a delightful experience, and choosing the right hostel can make your trip even more enjoyable. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, these hostels offer the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community.

Happy travels!
