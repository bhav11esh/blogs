The Best Hostels of Jodhpur

bhavesh July 6, 2024

Discovering the Vibrant Blue City of Jodhpur

Jodhpur, also known as the “”Blue City”” due to the blue-painted houses in the old town, is located in the state of Rajasthan, India. This vibrant city is famous for its imposing Mehrangarh Fort, built in the 15th century, as well as its bustling markets, delicious street food, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the narrow winding streets of the old town, shop for traditional handicrafts, and marvel at the stunning architecture of the various palaces and temples scattered throughout the city. Jodhpur offers a unique blend of history, culture, and hospitality, making it a must-visit destination for budget travelers.

The Best Hostels in Jodhpur for Backpackers

1. Moustache Jodhpur

  • Location: Jodhpur | 2.49 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: Moustache Jodhpur offers a vibrant and social atmosphere where guests can mingle and make new friends. The hostel’s decor is colorful and modern, and their focus on community activities and cultural exchanges make it a unique experience for travelers. Special features include rooftop views of the city and delicious local cuisine served in the hostel’s restaurant.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹373.29 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9.5/10.

2. Bob Hostel Jodhpur

  • Location: Jodhpur | 1.39 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: Bob Hostel Jodhpur has a vibrant and social atmosphere, perfect for meeting fellow travelers and exploring the city together. The hostel also offers cozy common areas, a rooftop terrace with a view of the fort, and regular events and activities for guests to enjoy.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹299 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9.2/10.

3. The Hosteller Heritage Palace, Jodhpur

  • Location: Jodhpur | 6.21 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: The Hosteller Heritage Palace offers a unique and traditional atmosphere with a focus on heritage and cultural experiences. Guests can enjoy the beautiful architecture of the palace, participate in cultural activities, and relax in the serene courtyard. Special features include traditional Rajasthani decor, organized city tours, and rooftop views of the city.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹666.49 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9/10.

Travel Tips

  1. Best time to travel: The best time to visit Jodhpur is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for exploring the city’s attractions like the Mehrangarh Fort and Umaid Bhawan Palace.

  2. Foods to try: Make sure to try the local Rajasthani cuisine in Jodhpur, including dishes like Dal Bati Churma, Mirchi Bada, and Ghevar. Don’t miss trying the famous Lassi and Makhania Lassi for a refreshing treat.

  3. Festive season to look out for: Visit Jodhpur during the colorful and vibrant Marwar Festival, held in October, to witness traditional music and dance performances, camel tattoo shows, and cultural programs that showcase the rich heritage of Rajasthan.
