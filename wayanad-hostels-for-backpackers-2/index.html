The Best Hostels in Wayanad

bhavesh July 3, 2024 best hostels

Mysore, nestled in the foothills of the Nilgiris, beckons travelers with its rich cultural heritage, stunning palaces, and vibrant markets. This captivating city, also known as Mysuru, offers a unique blend of history and modern life, making it an ideal destination. Mysore has something for every traveler. But where should you stay? We've rounded up the best hostels in Mysore to ensure you have a comfortable, budget-friendly, and memorable trip.

1. Zostel Plus Wayanad

  • Location: Meppadi (information on exact distance to city center unavailable)
  • Why we love it: Zostel Plus Wayanad offers a vibrant escape amidst nature. They feature comfortable dorms and private rooms, some boasting mountain views. The common areas are perfect for socializing, and the staff organizes activities like trekking and cultural experiences.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹719 per night on average.
  • Amenities:
    • Free Wi-Fi
    • Common areas
    • On-site cafe
    • Organized events and activities
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9.4/10.
  • Website: Check their website or booking platforms for more information and booking options.

2. Multiple Stories

  • Location: Meenangadi (information on exact distance to city center unavailable)
  • Why we love it: Multiple Stories offers a unique and tranquil stay. This homestay-style hostel provides comfortable dorm beds and private rooms, perfect for relaxation. The setting allows guests to connect with nature and experience local life.
  • Price Range: This is a budget-friendly option, with dorm beds typically costing around ₹487.5 per night.
  • Amenities:
    • Information online is limited, but likely includes basic amenities like Wi-Fi and common areas. Consider contacting the hostel directly for confirmation.
  • Score: With a perfect score of 100, this hostel boasts exceptional guest reviews.
  • Contact: Since detailed information online is limited, reach out to the hostel directly for details and booking options.

3. Adri Chembra

  • Location: Chembra (information on exact distance to city center unavailable)
  • Why we love it: Adri Chembra offers a comfortable and social stay close to nature. They have dorm rooms and private rooms, along with a common area for socializing. The location provides easy access to explore the nearby Chembra Peak and other natural attractions.
  • Price Range: Prices typically range around ₹900 per night.
  • Amenities:
    • Free Wi-Fi (likely, though confirmation might be needed)
    • Common area
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 6.6/10.
  • Website: Visit their website or booking platforms for details and booking options (website availability might be limited).

Tips for Hostel Travelers in Mysore

  • Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Wayanad is from October to May when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
  • Getting Around: Local buses and auto-rickshaws are affordable and readily available. Renting a scooter is also a great option to explore the scenic beauty of Wayanad at your own pace.
  • Must-See Attractions: Don’t miss the Edakkal Caves, Banasura Sagar Dam, and Chembra Peak. The Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is also a must-visit for nature enthusiasts

Exploring Wayanad is a delightful experience, and choosing the right hostel can make your trip even more enjoyable. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, these hostels offer the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community.

Happy travels!
