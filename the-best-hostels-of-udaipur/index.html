The Best Hostels of Udaipur

bhavesh July 6, 2024

Udaipur: A Budget Traveler’s Paradise

Udaipur, also known as the “”City of Lakes,”” is a picturesque city in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is famous for its stunning palaces, intricate architecture, colorful markets, and beautiful lakes. The city is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from all over the world with its rich history and vibrant culture.

Udaipur is known for its affordable accommodation options, including budget-friendly hostels that provide comfortable stays for travelers looking to explore the city on a budget. With its scenic beauty and cultural attractions, Udaipur is a must-visit destination for budget travelers seeking an authentic experience in India.

The Best Hostels in Udaipur for Backpackers

Hostel Mantra

  • Location: City | 2.48 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: Hostel Mantra offers a peaceful and zen atmosphere with a focus on relaxation and mindfulness. The hostel features a meditation room, yoga classes, and a vegetarian cafe. Guests love the tranquil environment and the opportunity to unwind and recharge.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹336.05 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9.8/10 from online data.

Tamasha Udaipur

  • Location: Udaipur | 2.44 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: Tamasha Udaipur offers a vibrant and lively atmosphere, with a focus on hosting events and fostering a sense of community among guests. Special features include themed parties, cultural activities, and a rooftop terrace with stunning views of the city.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹448.91 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9/10 from online data.

Dreamyard Udaipur

  • Location: Udaipur | 2.44 km away from city center
  • Why we love it: Dreamyard Udaipur offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere with beautiful gardens and cozy common areas. The hostel also offers activities such as yoga classes and cooking lessons. The staff is friendly and always ready to help guests have a memorable stay.
  • Price Range: Expect to pay ₹572.5 per night on average.
  • Rating: Guests typically rate this hostel around 9.6/10.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to travel: Visit Udaipur in the winter months from October to March when the weather is more pleasant and comfortable for sightseeing.
  • Must-try foods: Include dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, laal maas, and kachori. Don’t forget to try traditional Rajasthani sweets like ghewar and malpua.
  • Local festivals: Look out for the Mewar Festival in March/April, the Gangaur Festival in March/April, and the Shilpgram Crafts Fair in December for a glimpse of Udaipur’s rich culture and traditions.
